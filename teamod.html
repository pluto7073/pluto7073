Additions: Tea! All Kinds!
Craft a TeaLeaf with String and paper to get a tea bag
Craft The Tea bag with a glass bottle and a water bucket
to get tea. Tea does nothing but act like a bottle of
water on its own. If you craft tea with a notch apple
you can get Notch Tea, Which is like a better version of
A golden apple. You can also craft two other types of tea
bags. If you craft a dried tea leaf with paper, string, and
a gold melon to get a healing tea bag. Craft this with water,
and a glass bottle to get healing tea. When drank, this tea
will give you instant health and saturation for 1 Minute
Another kind of tea bag/tea is breakfast tea. This is
basically like the Coffee From my coffee mod. Craft the
tea bag by crafting dried tea leaves with string, paper,
and sugar to get a Breakfast tea bag. Craft this like normal
tea and you get the blue Breakfast tea. Tea leaves are grown
using tea seeds. Then the leaves are dried on a campfire to
get dried tea leaves
